Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How long does image generation take?

Ans. Generation speed depends on your chosen plan and quality settings. Standard quality usually takes 10-15 seconds. Pro and Ultimate users experience significantly faster processing times (3-5 seconds).

Q. What models do you use?

Ans. We utilize a private, fine-tuned version of a Diffusion Model (Lumi-Diffuser v3.0), optimized for the styles available in our selection menus.

Q. Can I use the generated images commercially?

Ans. Yes, all images generated on our Pro and Ultimate plans grant you full commercial rights. Free tier generations are for personal use only.

Q. How do I access the premium styles?

Ans. Premium styles and Ultra Quality settings require a subscription to either the Pro Creator or Ultimate Visionary plans. These options will unlock automatically once your subscription is active.
